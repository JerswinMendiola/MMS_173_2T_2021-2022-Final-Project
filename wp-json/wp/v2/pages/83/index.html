About the Creator<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
\n\n\n\n

Hello! My name is Jerswin Mendiola, the creator of a website named Mom’s Garden, a collection of photos from – my mother’s garden.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I gathered inspiration from the other project entries in this course, seeing that they went with a theme with their entries, and that they focused heavily on making interesting shots and variety.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

<\/span>
<\/div><\/div>\n\n\n\n

And while I like the idea of variety, I was also struck with the idea of sticking to one main theme for the whole project, and that’s when I came up with the idea to take photos only in the vicinity of my mother’s garden, which is fairly expansive.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

It forced me to be creative and find shots that don’t all just meld into each other, since the main subject in this photos would be fairly similar, I wanted every photo to feel at least unique in their style or story that they’re trying to tell.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

<\/span>
<\/div><\/div>\n\n\n\n

This limitation turned out to be a good challenge in terms of finding creative shots, as I had to look into angles and perspectives that I normally don’t look at when viewing the garden and see these hidden beauty angles that mother nature has brought us.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

<\/span>
<\/div><\/div>\n\n\n\n

Overall, I hope you enjoy these wonderful pictures of my mother’s garden and that hopefully, this gallery will grow twice or more in the future.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
